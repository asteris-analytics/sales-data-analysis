# Sales Data Analysis

## Key Insights

- Cosmetics generated the highest total profit ($92.7M).
- Clothes achieved the highest profit margin (67.2%), making it the most profitable category per dollar of revenue.
- January was the most profitable month, while March generated the highest total revenue.
- Andorra, Ukraine, and Malta were among the top contributors to total profit.
- Cosmetics combined both high profit margins and high total profit, making them the strongest overall product category.
- Office Supplies generated substantial profit despite lower margins, suggesting strong sales volume.
- Profitability varied significantly across product categories and countries.
